# Manulife (manulife)

Manulife Financial Corporation is a Canadian multinational insurance and financial services company providing life insurance, health insurance, wealth and asset management, banking, and group benefits. Manulife operates under the Manulife brand in Canada and Asia and through John Hancock Financial in the United States, with Manulife Bank of Canada and Manulife Investment Management as key subsidiaries. Manulife does not currently publish a public developer API or developer portal.
